Product Description


Surely, the ultimate test of any shoe would be to put them through their paces on the cricket pitch, with matches that are hours long and moments of play that happen at breakneck speed. You don't exactly want to get caught out there in something that amounts to a pair of glorified flip flops is what we're saying. With that in mind, we created these sneakers to get cricketers between the stumps as fast as possible and cushion their stance in the outfield. The breathable TPU and mesh upper is durable, and the toe area is reinforced for better protection. An EVA midsole ensures comfort with every step, while the rubber-plated outsole with removable spikes keeps you nimble and boosts bursts of energy when it's needed.
